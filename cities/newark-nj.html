Selling a Home in Newark: What You Should Know

Foreclosure Process in New Jersey

New Jersey uses a judicial foreclosure process, which typically takes 12 to 18 months from start to finish. If you're behind on payments, acting early gives you the most options — including selling before the bank forecloses.

Seller Disclosure Requirements

New Jersey requires sellers to complete a Seller's Disclosure Statement and provide a smoke detector/CO alarm certification. When you sell to Remedy Home Buyers, the disclosure process is straightforward — we buy as-is and handle any issues after closing.
