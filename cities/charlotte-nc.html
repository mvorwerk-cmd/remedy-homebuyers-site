Selling a Home in Charlotte: What You Should Know

Foreclosure Process in North Carolina

North Carolina uses a non-judicial foreclosure process, which typically takes 2 to 4 months from start to finish. If you're behind on payments, acting early gives you the most options — including selling before the bank forecloses.

Seller Disclosure Requirements

North Carolina requires sellers to complete a Residential Property and Owners' Association Disclosure Statement. When you sell to Remedy Home Buyers, the disclosure process is straightforward — we buy as-is and handle any issues after closing.
